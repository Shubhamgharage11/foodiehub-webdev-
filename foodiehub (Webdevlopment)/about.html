Who We Are

Crafting Memorable Meals, One Order at a Time

FoodieHub started in 2022 in the heart of Pune as a tiny kitchen run by two food-obsessed friends. Today, we serve over 10,000 happy customers every month with a menu that celebrates both Indian comfort food and global flavours — all prepared fresh, every single day.

We believe great food shouldn't be complicated. Our chefs use locally sourced ingredients, time-tested recipes, and a whole lot of love to make sure every bite is worth it.

Locally Sourced Produce Fresh vegetables and dairy from local Pune farms, delivered daily.
Cooked to Order Nothing sits under a heat lamp. Every dish is prepared after you order.
FSSAI Certified Kitchen We maintain the highest food safety and hygiene standards at all times.
