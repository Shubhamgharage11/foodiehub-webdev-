FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Our average delivery time is 25–30 minutes. This depends on your location and current order volume. During peak hours (lunch/dinner), it may take up to 40 minutes.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most areas in Pune including Deccan, Kothrud, Baner, Aundh, Hinjewadi, Viman Nagar, Koregaon Park, and more. Enter your pincode at checkout to confirm.

How do I apply a discount coupon?

On the cart/checkout page, you'll see a "Apply Coupon" field. Enter your code (e.g. WELCOME30, PIZZA2X) and click Apply. The discount will be deducted automatically.

Do you have vegetarian / vegan options?

Absolutely! A large portion of our menu is vegetarian and we're growing our vegan options. All veg items are clearly marked with a green "Veg" badge. Look for plant-based alternatives in every category.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery. Online payments are 100% secure via Razorpay.
